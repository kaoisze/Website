Tetris

What Is Tetris

Tetris is a simple game where you stack blocks or the official name, Tetriminoes that come in seven shapes. The version of the game I will be basing It off of will be Tetris 99, which you can play for free on the nintendo switch.


How to play Tetris 99

You are put in a battle royale version of Tetris, which you can attack players by using the right joystick on the nintendo switch. you move the peices by using the D-pad. To rotate the blocks, you have to press A, and If you want to hold the blocks, you press R on the switch.


If you like games that require quick thinking and puzzle solving, then this is your game.
