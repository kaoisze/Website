Genshin Impact

What the game Is about

Genshin Impact Is a JRPG game which you either play as Aether or Lumine, though you will change the name, after you pick on them

It starts off, by showing you a fight with a unkown god, which stops you and your sister called lumine.(Aether is supposed to be the main character) You both get seperated and then you get sranded in teyvat, and you meet paimon, you traveling companion, or emergency food.(thats the joke). They will then teach you how to fight and walk and many important things about how to play the game and your quest is to find your sister.
