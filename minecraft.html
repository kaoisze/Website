Minecraft

What Is Minecraft

Minecraft, as we have all heard at least once before Is a sandbox game where you can do anything in. You can build skyscrapers, get a pet, and even fly around. But before anything, we will need to learn how to play.


How to play

  • Movement – W, A, S, & D keys.
  • Jump – Spacebar.
  • Run – Double tap W.
  • Crouch/Sneak – Hold Shift.
  • Access inventory – E.
  • Use the mouse to look around.
  • To break blocks or attack, press or hold the left click on the mouse.
  • To use things, such as your crafting table when you make it, you press right click on it.
  • At night time, monsters will come and try to kill you, so either make a shelter, where you can live the night if you are not prepared to fight yet.
