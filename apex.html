Apex Legends


What Is Apex Legends

Apex Legends is a FPS battleroyale video game, which you goal is to be the last team/person left. YThe characters you choose have unqiue abilites, which will help you in the game.


Weapons

There are many weapons in this game ranging from shotguns, to snipers. These weapons vary in certain categories, which are Assault rifles, Sub machine guns, Light machine guns, Marksman weapons, Sniper rifles, Shotguns, and Pistols.
